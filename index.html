How ClearPath Works

Emergency Detection

Ambulance automatically broadcasts emergency signal with GPS, speed, and severity code to our secure backend.

Signal Preemption

Automatically creates green waves at upcoming intersections, clearing the path ahead of the ambulance.

Traffic Officer Alerts

Real-time notifications to traffic police with one-tap actions to clear lanes and manage traffic.

Driver Notifications

Push alerts to nearby drivers via navigation apps and connected vehicles to create safe corridors.

Predictive Routing

AI-powered route optimization considering traffic patterns, road conditions, and hospital capacity.

Analytics & Audit

Comprehensive logging and analytics for performance tracking and continuous improvement.
